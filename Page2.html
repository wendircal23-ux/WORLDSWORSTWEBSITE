On the upside, cities usually have more hospitals, clinics, and fitness options, making it easier to access medical care or stay active.

In contrast, countryside living feels calmer and healthier for the body and mind. Cleaner air, quiet surroundings, and open spaces reduce stress and improve lung health. People often get more physical activity through walking, gardening, or farm work, and sunlight helps boost vitamin D. The main downside is that medical facilities may be farther away in emergencies, but the overall environment supports a slower, more natural pace of life.
